<objective>
Build the complete broker and order management interface. Admin can create/edit broker profiles, manage broker status, create orders with vertical + credit score criteria, and control order lifecycle (start, pause, resume, complete, bonus toggle) through data tables with inline actions.

Purpose: Brokers and orders are the core entities the assignment engine operates on. Without them, there's nothing to match leads against. The admin needs full CRUD control over both.

Output: Working broker and order management pages with forms, data tables, and all lifecycle actions.
</objective>
